❓ Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I book a tour?

You can book directly through our website or via our booking partners. Just pick your tour, choose a date, and we’ll take care of the rest (no spell required).

2. Are the tours private or group-based?

All tours are private by default — it’s just you (and your chosen companions) with a dedicated guide. Want to bring a few friends? Great. Want it to be just the two of you? Even better.

3. What’s included in the tour price?

Each tour includes your private guide, customized storytelling, and plenty of insider knowledge. Some tours (like our foodie tour) also include tastings, which we’ll mention in the description.

4. How much walking is involved?

All tours are walking-based and cover about 1.5 to 3 hours of leisurely strolling with stops. Wear comfy shoes and dress for the weather — London is charmingly unpredictable.

5. Are the tours suitable for kids?

Yes! Most of our tours are family-friendly, especially the Harry Potter and London Highlights tours. For younger guests, we tweak the pace and stories. The Jack the Ripper tour is better suited to ages 14+ due to mature content.

6. Can you adapt the tour to my interests?

Absolutely. These tours are flexible — want more history? Less walking? A coffee stop mid-tour? Just let us know when you book, and we’ll tailor the experience.

7. What happens if it rains?

It’s London — rain is part of the magic. Tours run in all weather, but we’ll keep things cozy and adjust the route if needed. Bring an umbrella and a sense of adventure!

8. Can I cancel or reschedule?

Yes. We offer free rescheduling or cancellation up to 48 hours before the tour. Life happens — just give us a heads-up and we’ll make it work.

9. Where do the tours start and end?

Each tour has a central London meeting point (we’ll send exact details after booking). Ending locations vary slightly but are always in accessible areas with nearby transport links.

10. Do you offer gift cards or custom experiences?

We sure do. Whether it’s a birthday surprise, corporate treat, or anniversary adventure, we can create a custom tour or voucher that suits your needs. Just drop us a message.
