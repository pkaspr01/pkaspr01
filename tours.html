Our Unique Tours

Harry Potter in London: A Magical Movie Locations Tour

Step into the wizarding world and walk the real streets that brought Harry Potter’s London adventures to life. This tour takes you beyond King’s Cross — from the Ministry of Magic’s secret entrances to the alleyways that inspired Diagon Alley. Led by a local fan (and part-time Muggle), you’ll visit key filming locations, hear behind-the-scenes stories, and discover the surprising ways magic and London history overlap. We’ll stop for plenty of photo ops and maybe even share a few tips on how to disappear into a wall — legally. Whether you grew up with the books or just love a good story, this one’s for curious fans who want a deeper, fun look at Harry’s London.

Duration: Approx. 2.5 hours
Price: £190.00

Vintage London: Treasure Hunt Tour

Step back in time and wander through London’s quirkiest markets and hidden retro corners. This tour is a dream for vintage lovers, second-hand style hunters, and anyone who believes the best finds are the ones with a story. Led by a local thrifter (with a trained eye and a soft spot for 70s coats), we’ll stroll through iconic spots like Brick Lane, Soho’s secret shops, and the colorful stalls of Camden — with plenty of time to rummage. Expect unique fashion, antique oddities, vinyl records, and tales of London’s ever-changing style. No two stalls are the same, and no two tours are either.

Duration: Approx. 2.5 hours
Price: £170.00

London for First-Timers: Classic Highlights Tour

First time in London? We’ve got you. This tour is your friendly fast-track to everything iconic — Big Ben, Buckingham Palace, Tower Bridge, and the charming bits in between. With a local guide who knows the shortcuts, photo angles, and best times to dodge crowds, you’ll see the city’s biggest sights without feeling like you’re sprinting through a checklist. Along the way, we’ll chat royals, red buses, pub culture, and the weird little details that make London, well, London.

Duration: Approx. 3 hours
Price: £210.00

Tastes of London: Foodie Tour

Eat your way through the city — one delicious bite at a time. This food-focused walking tour is for curious eaters, street food fans, and anyone who believes good travel starts with good snacks. We’ll graze our way through London’s best food markets and hidden local gems, sampling everything from flaky pastries to global flavors (plus some classics like fish & chips, of course). Led by a London foodie who’s taste-tested it all, you’ll hear the stories behind the dishes and the communities that brought them here. Come hungry.

Duration: Approx. 2.5 hours
Price: £190.00

Jack the Ripper: Shadows of Victorian London

Slip into the fog of 1888 and follow in the footsteps of one of London’s most chilling true stories. This tour dives into the mystery of Jack the Ripper — with real locations, haunting theories, and a chilling atmosphere you won’t soon forget.

Led by a storyteller with a flair for the dramatic (and a healthy respect for Victorian detail), we’ll explore the narrow streets of Whitechapel and hear about the lives behind the headlines.

This isn’t just about the killer — it’s about the people, the times, and the lingering shadow of unsolved history.

⚠️ Not recommended for young children
🌙 Evening tours available

Duration: Approx. 2 hours
Price: £170.00
